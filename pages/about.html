Pancakes!

About

Our website features delicious pancake recipes for you to try! It is split into the categories 'Meaty', 'Veggie', and 'Vegan' to cater for all diet preferences.

We use a range of ingredients to add variety to our pancakes; switching plain flour for spelt flour and using coconut cream instead of cow's milk cream for example.

If you're feeling adventurous, venture away from what you would normally for and try a different type of pancake!

Don't forget to share the recipes with your friends so everyone can enjoy them!
